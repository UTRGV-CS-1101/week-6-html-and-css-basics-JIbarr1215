The Dark, The Scary, & The Confused

A fanpage celebrating four iconic Pokémon

Why we love these Pokémon

Each of these Pokémon brings something unique: spooky trickery from Gengar, elegant power from Absol, comic relief from Psyduck, and mythic scale from Giratina. Fans collect art, learn lore, and celebrate their favorite abilities.

Pokémon Profiles

Gengar

Type: Ghost / Poison

Lore: Gengar is a shadowy trickster that lurks in darkness and feeds on the fear of others. It is often portrayed as playing pranks or hiding in people's shadows. In the Pokédex, Gengar is known to appear where shadows are long and to cause feelings of chill or unease.

Notable: Frequently associated with haunted places and mischievous behavior.

Absol

Type: Dark

Lore: Absol is known as the disaster Pokémon because it can sense impending calamities with its horn. Villagers sometimes mistake its appearance for the cause of misfortune, but Absol typically shows up before disasters to warn people. It carries a solemn, mysterious air.

Notable: Associated with natural disaster warnings and solitary behavior.

Psyduck

Type: Water (often linked to Psychic power)

Lore: Psyduck suffers from chronic headaches that hide its latent psychic potential. When its headache intensifies, Psyduck can unleash powerful, uncontrolled psychic phenomena. Its confused, vacant expression is part of its charm, and it often provides comic relief in the anime and games.

Notable: Unpredictable psychic outbursts triggered by severe headaches.

Giratina

Type: Ghost / Dragon

Lore: Giratina is a Legendary Pokémon tied to alternate dimensions and the balance of the world. It is said to dwell in a distorted realm beyond the regular world, and its presence is linked to antimatter and the distortion of reality. Giratina's scale and mythic nature make it a figure of awe and mystery.

Notable: Legendary status and strong ties to alternate dimensions and balance.
